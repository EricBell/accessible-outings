Making the World More Accessible

Accessible Outings Finder helps people with mobility challenges discover and enjoy accessible venues in their community. We believe everyone deserves to explore, discover, and participate in the world around them.

Our Mission

We're committed to breaking down barriers and creating a more inclusive world. Our platform connects people with accessible venues, providing detailed information about wheelchair accessibility, parking, restrooms, and other essential features.

Whether you're planning a family outing, a romantic dinner, or exploring a new city, we help you find places where everyone can participate fully and comfortably.

What We Offer

Detailed Accessibility Info

Comprehensive accessibility ratings and features for each venue, including wheelchair access, parking, restrooms, and seating options.

Location-Based Search

Find accessible venues near you with customizable search radius. Get directions and plan your visit with confidence.

Community Reviews

Read and share real experiences from other visitors. Help build a community of accessible travel knowledge.
